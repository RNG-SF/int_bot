## 🌷 Our Greatest Enemies

The hardest battles are rarely against other people.

Most of the time...

they happen inside ourselves.

Two of the strongest are:

### 🌊 Hope

Hope is beautiful.

But when it's placed on someone who never promised anything...

it slowly becomes a burden.

Sometimes we don't miss the person.

We miss the future we imagined with them.

---

### 🍂 Ego

Ego isn't always loud.

Sometimes...

it's the quiet voice refusing to accept reality.

It keeps us waiting.

Keeps us chasing.

Keeps us believing that one more try will change everything.

Even when deep inside...

we already know the answer.

---

## 🌼 So...

If today feels heavier than yesterday...

Rest.

Drink some water.

Look at the sky.

Take one deep breath.

Then continue when you're ready.

Because...

> **Moving slowly is still moving forward.**
